Classic Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is a crowd-pleaser, with layers of rich meat sauce, cheesy goodness, and perfectly cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place 4 lasagna noodles on top. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese, followed by a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
